# PRD: Epic 5.1.A - GTM Strategy, Positioning & Messaging Matrices

## 1. Overview
**Epic:** 5.1.A
**Phase:** 5 (Launch & Growth)
**Owner:** Product Manager
**Status:** Draft / Pending Kickoff

Following the successful hardening and completion of Phase 4 (including Epic 4.4.C E2E testing), Epic 5.1.A kicks off the Launch & Growth phase (Sprint 5.1). This epic focuses on defining the exact Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy, product positioning, and messaging matrices necessary to successfully launch AgencyOS to the public.

## 2. Objectives
*   Establish clear, segmented product positioning for target audiences (startup founders, DevOps leads, marketing agencies).
*   Define cross-channel messaging matrices (Twitter, Reddit, Bilibili, Xiaohongshu, etc.).
*   Create a unified Value Proposition to drive launch collateral generation in subsequent epics.

## 3. Scope
*   **In Scope:**
    *   Target Audience Profiles & Personas definition.
    *   Messaging Matrix mapped to primary launch channels.
    *   Core Value Propositions & Elevator Pitches.
    *   Structuring the launch narrative and timeline.
*   **Out of Scope:**
    *   Actual marketing asset creation (Handled in Epic 5.1.B).
    *   Production deployment and infrastructure (Handled in Epics 5.1.C and Sprint 5.2).

## 4. Requirements & Deliverables
1.  **GTM Strategy Blueprint:** A master strategy detailing the launch timeline, channel prioritization, and engagement tactics.
2.  **Positioning Statement:** Core product messaging focusing on the pillars: "Automate Everything," "Unified AI Agents," and "Secure & Production-Ready."
3.  **Messaging Matrix:** A matrix mapping target personas to their respective pain points, the AgencyOS solutions, and channel-specific marketing angles.

## 5. Dependencies & Validation Gates
*   **Requirement:** This Epic must pass a Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Validation Gate (Stakeholder review and approval of GTM positioning) before Content Creation (Epic 5.1.B) begins.
*   **Ecosystem Review:** Ensure compliance with Ecosystem Review Board policies when setting monetization or marketplace GTM strategies.

## 6. Next Steps
1.  Assign `Business Strategist` and `Trend Researcher` to gather final market trends and competitive intel.
2.  Draft final positioning documents (`docs/core/GTM_Strategy.md`).
3.  Present GTM Strategy for human approval.
