# Business & Strategy Pod

## Pod Name
Business & Strategy Pod

## Description
A specialized cross-functional group designed to analyze market trends, financial data, and competitive landscapes, translating those insights into actionable business strategy and requirements. This pod leads the strategic planning phase before handing off requirements to the technical teams.

## Members
* **Trend Researcher:** Market intel & competitive analysis
* **Analytics Reporter:** Data analysis & visualization
* **Financial Analyst:** Financial modeling & business intelligence
* **Executive Summary Generator:** C-suite reporting
* **Business Strategist:** High-level planning and mode guidance

## Triggers / Activation Context
* When a new market opportunity requires strategic validation and business modeling.
* During quarterly business reviews to translate financial and analytical findings into business objectives.
* When high-level strategic direction is required for new product features or pivots.

## Expected Outputs
* Comprehensive business requirement documents (BRDs).
* Executive reports on market trends and financial forecasts.
* Strategic insights and validated business objectives prepared for technical handoff.

## Workflow
1. **Discovery & Research:** The Trend Researcher and Financial Analyst gather market and financial data.
2. **Analysis:** The Analytics Reporter processes the data and visualizes key metrics.
3. **Strategy Formulation:** The Business Strategist defines the high-level objectives based on the analysis.
4. **Reporting:** The Executive Summary Generator compiles findings into a cohesive C-suite report.
5. **Technical Handoff:** The Business Strategist packages the finalized strategic insights, business objectives, and BRDs, handing them off to the Tech & AI Capabilities Pod for feasibility analysis and implementation planning.
