# Tech & AI Capabilities Pod

## Pod Name
Tech & AI Capabilities Pod

## Description
A specialized cross-functional group responsible for the technical feasibility, architectural design, and implementation of data infrastructure and intelligent systems. This pod receives business requirements from the Business & Strategy Pod and iterates on technical solutions.

## Members
* **AI Engineer:** Intelligent System Development
* **Software Architect:** System Design
* **Data Engineer:** Data Infrastructure

## Triggers / Activation Context
* When business requirements and strategic insights are handed off from the Business & Strategy Pod.
* When launching data-driven products that require integration between AI models and underlying data infrastructure.
* When technical feasibility studies are required for new strategic initiatives.

## Expected Outputs
* System architecture designs tied directly to business requirements.
* Data pipelines connecting data sources with AI models.
* Technical proofs-of-concept for proposed AI features.

## Workflow
1. **Requirement Ingestion:** The Software Architect and AI Engineer receive inputs (BRDs, strategic insights) from the Business & Strategy Pod.
2. **Technical Planning:** The Software Architect designs the required systems and overall architecture to support the strategic objectives.
3. **Infrastructure Setup:** The Data Engineer designs and implements the data infrastructure and pipelines necessary for the initiative.
4. **AI Integration:** The AI Engineer develops and integrates intelligent features, iterating on solutions aligned with the business goals.
5. **Feedback Loop:** The pod continuously iterates on technical solutions, providing feedback on technical feasibility back to the Business Strategist if adjustments to the business requirements are necessary.
