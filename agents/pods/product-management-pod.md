# Product Management Pod

## Pod Name
Product Management Pod

## Description
This pod bridges the gap between business strategy and technical execution. Its mission is to oversee the collaboration between the Business Strategy Pod and the Tech & AI Pod, manage timelines, and ensure strategic objectives are translated into actionable, prioritized sprints.

## Members
* **Product Manager:** Owns the product lifecycle, translates business requirements into product specifications, and ensures alignment with overall strategy.
* **Project Shepherd:** Manages execution, tracks dependencies, facilitates communication between pods, and ensures timelines are met.
* **Sprint Prioritizer:** Evaluates technical input and business value to scope sprints, prioritize the backlog, and optimize resource allocation.

## Triggers / Activation Context
When a Business Requirements Document (BRD) is finalized by the Business Strategy Pod and requires technical scoping, sprint planning, and execution oversight by the Tech & AI Pod.

## Expected Outputs
* Prioritized sprint plans and scoped technical tasks.
* Execution timelines and dependency tracking matrix.
* Progress reports and unblocked workflows between business and technical teams.

## Workflow
1. **Intake and Review:** The Product Manager receives the finalized Business Requirements Document (BRD) from the Business Strategy Pod and reviews it for completeness and strategic alignment.
2. **Technical Consultation:** The pod engages the Tech & AI Pod to assess technical feasibility, estimate effort, and break down the BRD into actionable technical components.
3. **Sprint Scoping:** The Sprint Prioritizer uses the technical input and business priorities to structure the work into manageable sprints, prioritizing high-value deliverables.
4. **Execution Management:** The Project Shepherd coordinates the daily execution, tracking progress across the Tech & AI Pod, removing blockers, and managing cross-pod communication.
5. **Review and Handoff:** The pod oversees the transition of completed sprints, ensuring deliverables meet the original business requirements before final sign-off.
